CLIPS FOLDER
============

This folder is for your CONTENT CLIPS thumbnails.

- Put your clip thumbnail images here (clip1.png, clip2.png, etc.)
- These should be 9:16 aspect ratio (VERTICAL - like TikTok/Instagram)
- Examples: 1080x1920, 720x1280
- These WILL have YouTube links attached to them

See HOW_TO_ADD_CLIPS_AND_THUMBNAILS.txt for detailed instructions.
